# Key Israeli Legislation Index

## Overview
This index covers the most commonly referenced Israeli laws, organized by area.
For each law, the Hebrew name, year of enactment, and key sections are provided.

## Constitutional / Basic Laws (Chukei Yesod)

### Basic Law: Human Dignity and Liberty (1992)
- Hebrew: Chok Yesod: Kvod HaAdam VeCheruto
- Key sections: Saif 2 (protection of life, body, dignity), Saif 7 (privacy rights)
- Significance: Quasi-constitutional status, basis for judicial review
- Amended: 1994 (limitation clause added)

### Basic Law: Freedom of Occupation (1992)
- Hebrew: Chok Yesod: Chofesh HaIsuk
- Key sections: Saif 3 (right to engage in any occupation)
- Significance: Economic freedom protection

### Basic Law: The Knesset (1958)
- Hebrew: Chok Yesod: HaKnesset
- Key sections: Election law, Knesset immunity, basic parliamentary rules

### Basic Law: The Government (2001)
- Hebrew: Chok Yesod: HaMemshala
- Key sections: Formation of government, PM powers, ministerial responsibility

## Contract Law

### Contracts Law (General Part) 1973
- Hebrew: Chok HaChozim (Chelek Klali)
- Key sections:
  - Saif 1: Contract formation (offer and acceptance)
  - Saif 12: Good faith in negotiations (tom lev)
  - Saif 14-15: Mistake and fraud (taut ve'ona'a)
  - Saif 17-19: Duress and undue influence
  - Saif 25: Contract interpretation
  - Saif 39: Good faith in performance
- Note: No consideration requirement (unlike common law systems)

### Contracts (Remedies for Breach) Law 1970
- Hebrew: Chok HaChozim (Taknaot BeShal Hafarat Choze)
- Key sections: Enforcement (afikhat choze), cancellation (bitul), damages (pitzuim)

### Sale Law 1968
- Hebrew: Chok HaMecher
- Key sections: Seller obligations, buyer obligations, defects (mumim)

### Gift Law 1968
- Hebrew: Chok HaMatana

### Guarantee Law 1967
- Hebrew: Chok HaArvut

## Employment Law

### Employment Law Overview
Israeli employment law is heavily protective of employees and consists of
multiple separate statutes:

### Severance Pay Law 1963
- Hebrew: Chok Pitzuei Piturin
- Key sections:
  - Saif 1: Right to severance after 1 year of employment
  - Saif 12: Calculation (1 month salary per year)
  - Saif 14: Section 14 arrangement (pension fund substitution)
- Critical: Section 14 arrangement is now near-universal for new employees

### Hours of Work and Rest Law 1951
- Hebrew: Chok Shaot Avoda UMenucha
- Key sections: Maximum work hours, overtime, rest day (yom menucha)
- Standard: 42 hours/week maximum

### Annual Leave Law 1951
- Hebrew: Chok Chufsha Shnatit
- Key sections: Minimum vacation days by seniority

### Sick Pay Law 1976
- Hebrew: Chok Dmei Machala
- Key sections: Sick leave entitlement, medical certificate requirements

### Minimum Wage Law 1987
- Hebrew: Chok Schar Minimum
- Updated periodically by government order

### Equal Employment Opportunity Law 1988
- Hebrew: Chok Shivion Hizdamniut BaAvoda
- Key sections: Prohibited discrimination grounds, burden of proof

### Prevention of Sexual Harassment Law 1998
- Hebrew: Chok Meni'at Hatrada Minit
- Key sections: Definition, employer obligations, complaint procedures

### Notice of Dismissal and Resignation Law 2001
- Hebrew: Chok Hoda'a Mukdemet LePiturin ULeHitpitarut

## Corporate Law

### Companies Law 1999
- Hebrew: Chok HaChevarot
- Key sections:
  - Part 3: Incorporation and registration
  - Part 5: Share capital
  - Part 6: Company management (directors, officers)
  - Part 7: Transactions with interested parties (ba'alei inyan)
  - Part 8: Mergers and acquisitions
  - Part 10: Dissolution and liquidation
- Registration: Companies Registrar (Rasham HaChevarot) at Ministry of Justice

### Partnerships Ordinance (New Version) 1975
- Hebrew: Pkudat HaShutafiot

### Cooperative Societies Ordinance
- Hebrew: Pkudat HaAgudot HaShitufiot

## Real Estate Law

### Land Law 1969
- Hebrew: Chok HaMikrkain
- Key sections: Ownership (baalut), leasehold, easements (zchut ma'avar)
- Land registration: Tabu (Land Registry)

### Planning and Building Law 1965
- Hebrew: Chok HaTichnun VeHaBniya
- Key sections: Building permits, zoning, planning committees

### Sale of Apartments Law (Assurance of Investments) 1974
- Hebrew: Chok Mecher Dirot (Havtachat Hashka'ot)
- Key sections: Developer guarantees for apartment buyers

### Tenant Protection Law 1972
- Hebrew: Chok Haganat HaDayar
- Key sections: Protected tenancy, rent control (limited application today)

### Rent and Borrowing Law 1971
- Hebrew: Chok HaSechirut VeHaShee'la

## Consumer Protection

### Consumer Protection Law 1981
- Hebrew: Chok Haganat HaTzarchan
- Key sections: Misleading advertising, cooling-off period, unfair terms

### Standard Contracts Law 1982
- Hebrew: Chok HaChozim HaAchidim
- Key sections: Unconscionable terms, Standard Contracts Tribunal

## Tort Law

### Torts Ordinance (New Version) 1968
- Hebrew: Pkudat HaNezikin (Nusach Chadash)
- Key sections:
  - Negligence (rashlanut)
  - Strict liability (achrayut muchletet)
  - Defamation provisions (now separate law)
  - Damage assessment

### Defamation Law 1965
- Hebrew: Chok Isur Lashon HaRa

- Hebrew: Chok Mas Shevach Mikrkain
